It is a system that assists visually impaired people in their everyday lives by providing guidance and warning functionalities. It is hardware powered by artificial intelligence and a mobile application. It detects objects close to the person by feeding its artificial intelligence with the data collected from its laser sensor and camera. The mobile application warns the person aloud about the objects close to them.
